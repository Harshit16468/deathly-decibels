About Us

Welcome to Deathly Decibels - your ultimate destination for all things dark and macabre in the world of music. Our platform is designed to showcase the most haunting and eerie melodies from different genres of music, and provide a community for fans of the darker side of music to connect and share their passion.

However, that's not all we're about. While our platform is themed around dark and eerie music, we also celebrate other genres of music that are not necessarily considered "dark". Our goal is to provide a space where music enthusiasts can come together to discover and share their favorite music, regardless of genre.


Our rating system is a key feature of our platform that allows you to contribute to our community playlist of deathly decibels, as well as other playlists that reflect different genres of music. You can rate songs based on their overall quality, their relevance to a particular genre, and their ability to evoke emotions. As you rate more songs, you'll see your contribution to our playlists grow and evolve, reflecting your unique taste and perspective.
We would like to take this opportunity to thank you for being a part of the Deathly Decibels community. Your support and contribution to our platform is what keeps us going.
