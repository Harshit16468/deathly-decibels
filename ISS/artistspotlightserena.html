Selena Gomez

Selena Gomez is an American singer, actress, and producer who rose to fame as a child star on the Disney Channel. She was born on July 22, 1992, in Grand Prairie, Texas. Gomez has pursued a successful music career, releasing several albums and hit singles. Some of her popular songs include Come & Get It, The Heart Wants What It Wants, and Lose You to Love Me.
